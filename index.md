## About Me

Hi :wave:! I am Haoran Li (李浩然), a junior undergraduate student from [Sichuan University](https://en.scu.edu.cn/) and currently an exchange student at [The Hong Kong Polytechnic University](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/).
